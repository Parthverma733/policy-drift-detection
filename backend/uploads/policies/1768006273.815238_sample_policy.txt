Rural Education Support Scheme

Policy Document

This policy aims to provide comprehensive educational support to rural districts with low literacy rates.

Target Groups:
- Priority districts with literacy rate below 70%
- Districts with population above 50,000

Policy Requirements:
- Minimum coverage percentage of 80% for priority districts
- Minimum fund utilization of 90% for priority districts
- Maximum monthly variance of 10% to ensure consistent implementation

Temporal Rules:
- Reporting should be done monthly
- Consistency checks should be performed on a monthly basis

This policy is effective from April 2023 and will continue until March 2026.
